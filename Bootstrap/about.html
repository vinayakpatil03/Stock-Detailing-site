About Us

In the year 2000, Bharat Agrochem established its agrochemicals manufacturing unit in Bihar, marking the beginning of a journey that has since seen the company develop a reputation for excellence and expertise. Despite being a relatively small enterprise, our extensive knowledge and experience enable us to offer a wide range of high-quality products in various formulations, including liquid, wettable powder, granule, and dust.

One of our biggest strengths is our ability to provide targeted solutions to crop pest problems through our expert knowledge of pesticide selection and application. This competitive edge has helped us stand out from our competitors, even when offering the same products as many other companies.

Our marketing operations span over 11 states in India, with offices and warehouses strategically located throughout the country. With a renewed focus on farmer development work, we aim to expand our business footing in eastern India and neighboring countries, centered on the East. We are committed to adding value to our products in the market and for our customers, a tradition that has been fundamental to our success.

Our business model is rooted in understanding local cultural practices and the needs of end-consumers, allowing us to identify the best fit for our products. Our field development, sales, marketing, and regulatory science competencies have been honed over the years, enabling us to communicate effectively with our partners in the trade.

We take pride in our diverse product portfolio, which includes fungicides, herbicides, insecticides, and plant growth promoters. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with sophisticated equipment, ensuring the highest standards of quality and efficiency. As an ISO 9001: 2008 certified company, we are committed to upholding the strictest quality standards.

Our managing director brings over thirty-seven years of experience in specialty crop protection, a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and expertise. At Bharat Agrochem, we look forward to continuing to serve our customers and partners and expanding our footprint across India.
