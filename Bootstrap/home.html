ABOUT BHARAT AGROCHEM

For over 16 years, our company has been dedicated to manufacturing and marketing a wide range of agricultural products including fungicides, herbicides, insecticides, and plant growth promoters. Our commitment to providing high-quality and effective solutions to farmers and growers has made us a trusted partner in the industry..

Our team of experts is constantly researching and developing innovative products to address the evolving needs of the agricultural sector. We use only the best ingredients and adhere to strict quality control standards to ensure that our products deliver the best possible results.

WHY CHOOSE US

At our company, we have a deep-rooted passion for agriculture and take great pride in contributing to the advancements in technology that have transformed the industry over the last century.

When you choose us, you can be confident that you are working with a company that is deeply committed to sustainable agriculture, fair trade practices, and the highest standards of quality and integrity. We look forward to the opportunity to work with you and to continue building a brighter future for agriculture and our planet.



Director's Message

At Bharat Agrochem, we value the importance of building lasting relationships with our customers. We understand that excellent service is key to achieving this goal. That’s why we strive to provide the best possible service to our customers, each and every time.

To remain competitive in our industry, we have plans to become a larger, stronger, and more versatile company. Our initial goal was to provide quality products to the eastern region of India. However, we have since developed the competence to manage synthesis and formulation with reliable suppliers throughout India. Our supply chain is efficient and services customers in 11 states of Eastern India and Nepal. We are continuously learning and improving our regulatory skills to apply them everywhere.

We take pride in contributing to the agricultural industry and the positive changes that technology has brought about over the last century. Our passion for agriculture, both the science and business of it, runs deep within our company.
